- **If you lose your token, the data is unrecoverable.** We **cannot** and **will not** help recover it.
- **If your token is compromised**, the only secure action is to:
  1. Delete the encrypted video from all platforms
  2. Re-encode the original file (which will generate a new token and different visual output)
